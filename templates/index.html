About

Platform4's wonderful neighbor, the Limfjord, has long been flowing by quietly and uninterrupted. This will certainly change during Wet Art Workshop Week, where Copenhagen-based artistic group Illutron is coming to Aalborg to interact with Platform4's vibrant community in a collaborative workshop on interactive floating art.

The workshop, which is open to everybody, will run for almost an entire week and focus on building artistic constructions in the water. Floating, moving, sailing, submerged or whatever - we'll know along the way. Come join us and be a part of finishing the plan to create wet art! You can participate all days, some days or one day.

Participation in the workshop is free! (no need to participate all days, you can easily just be there one or more days).

Platform 4

Platform4 is a user-driven multi-purpose venue that experiments with new technological and digital elements in combination with artistic and creative genres ranging from music, theatre, contemporary art, design, architecture and more.
